About Lanora Gold

Lanora Gold Trading LLC, based in Al Ras, Deira, is a trusted wholesale supplier of gold, silver, platinum, and palladium. We offer 24K gold bars and coins in top purities, as well as 24K chains and bangles.

As authorized distributors of premium brands like Pamp and Valcambi, plus trusted local names, we provide the best prices, authentic products, and excellent customer service.

Our walk-in showroom welcomes all customers — no appointment needed. Whether you're an investor, jeweller, collector, or business, we're here to serve you with transparency and trust.
